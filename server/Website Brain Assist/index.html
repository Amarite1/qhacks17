BrainAssist has 3 major features

Our mission is "To work towards a world which nobody suffers in silence, and building social support systems for those in need."

Automated Checkups

BrainAssist tracks users progress and allows them to self report the state of their day.

Help Line Support

Brain Assist will provide information on where people can seek help near them.

The Epidemiology of Mental Health (Under Construction)

It is important to study the epidemiology of mental health issues, as it relates to geophysical location, so that we may support as many people as we can, and make educated public health decisions.
